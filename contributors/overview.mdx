# MCPBench Contributors

MCPBench is a community-driven project that brings together researchers, developers, and AI practitioners from around the world. Our contributors are the backbone of this benchmark, creating tasks, improving evaluation methods, and advancing the field of AI agent evaluation.

## How to Contribute

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Community">
    Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/mcpbench) to connect with other contributors and get help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Contribution">
    - Create new benchmark tasks
    - Improve existing tasks
    - Help with documentation
    - Contribute to infrastructure
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Guidelines">
    Read our [contribution guidelines](/contributors/guidelines) to understand our standards and processes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Work">
    Submit your contributions through GitHub issues and pull requests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Feedback">
    Work with the community to refine and improve your contributions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
